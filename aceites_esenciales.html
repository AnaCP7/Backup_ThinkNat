ESSENTIAL OILS

Essential oils contain numerous natural chemical compounds from the plant from which they are extracted and which are very beneficial for different purposes, such as stimulating the nervous system, relieving states of anxiety and depression, digestive and circulatory problems, etc.

ROSEMARY ESSENTIAL OIL

It is a great tonic, helps to mobilise fats, helps with fluid retention, activates the circulation and eliminates toxins. It also has an anti-rheumatic and anti-inflammatory effect, combating muscular pain and cramps. It is also indicated for respiratory conditions, in the treatment of colds, flu and asthma.

OREGANO ESSENTIAL OIL

Oregano essential oil is a good general physical, mental and sexual stimulant and tonic. It also protects against a broad spectrum of infections, such as viruses and fungi. It is a good ally in cases of respiratory infections and helps to purify the environment and surfaces.

THYME ESSENTIAL OIL

This is one of the most versatile essential oils available. It is used both in cosmetics and in health care. It has therapeutic properties and is ideal for fighting infections. It prevents the reduction of the defences and helps to treat chronic illnesses. It is also indicated to treat acne and boils.
