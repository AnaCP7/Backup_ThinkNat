SUPPLEMENTATION

Dietary supplements provide vitamins, minerals and other nutrients to the body to balance their levels with those of the food we eat. They have many benefits, such as boosting health and energy, supporting the immune system and reducing the risk of disease, improving physical and mental performance, among others.

Vitamin C

Is a nutrient that acts as an antioxidant and can help reduce the impact of oxidative stress. It also contributes to collagen production, a stronger immune system and stronger bones and joints

Turmeric

Reduces inflammation and helps heal wounds. Helps protect against a variety of diseases, such as rheumatoid arthritis, cardiovascular disease, dementia and some cancers.

Reishi

Reishi is a mushroom with multiple health-promoting properties. It is mainly indicated to strengthen the immune system and to work in the anti-inflammatory processes of the body.
