MEDICINAL PLANTS


Medicinal plants are known worldwide for their natural properties, as they help to alleviate, prevent and treat certain health problems at home. In fact, the World Health Organisation, in its Strategic Plan 2014-2023, points out the importance of promoting and including the use of natural medicines in the health systems of its member countries. There are hundreds of medicinal plants that are beneficial to health. Here is a selection of them

Lavender

Suitable for nervousness, anxiety and insomnia. Also for burns, skin lesions and insect bites.

Calendula

Indicated for skin and mucous membrane inflammations, dermatitis, burns, gastroduodenal ulcers and gastritis.

Arnica

Indicated for dislocations, bruises, haematomas, muscular or joint disorders, as well as an antiseptic for wounds.
